Chromatographic instrumentation has become a necessary laboratory tool for the analysis of all forms of active ingredients used in the formulation of natural health and dietary supplements. Through the use of technology such as High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Gas Chromatography (GC), sponsors can be confident that products manufactured meet label claims.
