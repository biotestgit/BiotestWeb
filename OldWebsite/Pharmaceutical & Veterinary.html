Biotest’s focus is on chemical analysis of pharmaceutical raw materials and finished products.

This includes full compendial analysis; client quality standards, client methods and Biotest’s validated methods.
   

Biotest Analytical Services provides analysis of active ingredients and marker compounds for QA/QC, purity contents, analysis of raw materials, extracts, intermediates, product release, finished products including solid dose and gel capsules, identification of impurities and active ingredients, independent method validation, method development, quality control, product registration and storage stability testing. Phoenix Analytical Services also runs analyses for vitamins, natural products, and nutritional products.
